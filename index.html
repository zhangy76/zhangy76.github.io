Yufei Zhang
I am a Senior AI Research Scientist at ByteDance Inc, working with the PICO team.
I aim to build Human-Embodied AI capable of perceiving, understanding, and emulating human behavior. My research integrates Physics (e.g., biomechanics) and Probabilistic Modeling (e.g., uncertainty estimation) into scalable data-driven systems.
