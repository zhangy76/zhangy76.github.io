About Me

I'm a PhD student in the Intelligent Systems Lab at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), where I was supervised by Prof. Qiang Ji. Previously, I received a M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Rochester and B.E. from Xi'an Jiaotong University.

My research interests are machine learning and computer vision, with focuses on probabilistic deep learning, physics-informed deep learning, and monocular 3D human body and hand reconstruction.
